About Me

Hello! I'm Ryan Chen, a recent Computer Science graduate from Boston University now based in New York City. My journey in tech began with a curiosity about how things work, which evolved into a passion for creating meaningful digital experiences.

Through my academic projects, I've explored diverse areas from AI systems to computer graphics. What excites me most is the intersection of technology and creativity - whether that's building intelligent game algorithms or developing visual computing solutions.

When approaching problems, I value clean, thoughtful solutions over complexity. My experience ranges from developing a Tetris AI that learns through reinforcement to creating 3D rendering pipelines from scratch. Each project has taught me the importance of both technical rigor and creative problem-solving.

Outside of coding, you'll find me:

  • Exploring NYC's vibrant tech and startup scene
  • Experimenting with creative coding and digital art
  • Learning about new developments in AI and graphics
  • Checking out the local food scene (always on the hunt for great ramen)

I'm currently looking for opportunities where I can contribute my technical skills while continuing to grow as a developer. If you're working on something interesting or just want to connect, I'd love to hear from you!
